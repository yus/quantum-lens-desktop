# UQD Quantum Lens — Unlimited Quantum Divergence

## How It Works
- **Lens Mode**: Smooth optical low-pass filter (denoiser)
- **Barrier Mode**: Divergent barrier ∫1/|x|^p dx (spectral compressor)

## Aalener Optical Formulas
- Fresnel reflection: `R = ((n1-n2)/(n1+n2))²`
- Material transmission: `T = (T₀/100)^(d/10)`

## Divergent Integral
When `p = 1.0`, the transfer function becomes `1/|f - f₀|` — infinite slope at cutoff.
